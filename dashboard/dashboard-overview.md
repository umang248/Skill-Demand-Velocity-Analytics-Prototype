## Dashboard Overview

The dashboard is designed to analyze skill adoption trends across different job roles and identify emerging skills using the **Skill Demand Velocity** framework.

The report consists of four main visualizations that help users explore skill demand patterns and workforce trends.

### 1. Velocity by Skill

This horizontal bar chart displays the average velocity of skills for the selected job role.

**What it shows:**

* Skills experiencing the highest growth in adoption.
* Emerging skills that are gaining demand faster than others.
* Relative comparison of skill momentum within a role.

**How to use it:**

* Select a job role from the filter panel.
* View the top skills ranked by their velocity score.
* Higher velocity indicates a faster increase in employer demand.

---

### 2. Job Role Filter

The job role slicer allows users to analyze specific professions individually.

**What it does:**

* Filters all dashboard visuals simultaneously.
* Enables role-specific analysis.
* Supports comparison of skill trends across different career paths.

**Examples:**

* Data Engineer
* Business Analyst
* AI Prompt Engineer
* Fintech Engineer
* Market Research Analyst

This helps users understand which skills are becoming important within a particular profession.

---

### 3. Skill Velocity Details Table

This table provides a detailed breakdown of skill velocity metrics.

**Columns:**

* **Job Role** – The selected profession.
* **Skill** – Individual skill associated with the role.
* **Average Velocity** – Average monthly change in adoption rate.

**Purpose:**

* Allows deeper investigation beyond the summary chart.
* Helps identify specific skills contributing to workforce shifts.
* Enables users to sort and compare skills based on growth rates.

---

### 4. Adoption Rate by Month Matrix

This matrix visualizes how frequently a skill appears in job postings across different months.

**What it shows:**

* Monthly adoption percentages for each skill.
* Historical demand patterns.
* Changes in employer requirements over time.

**How to interpret:**

* Higher percentages indicate greater adoption of a skill in job postings.
* Increasing values across months may signal an emerging skill.
* Stable values indicate consistently demanded skills.

This visual serves as the foundation for calculating Skill Demand Velocity by tracking how skill adoption changes over time.

---

## Key Insight

Rather than focusing only on the most common skills, this dashboard emphasizes **skill momentum and emerging trends**. By combining monthly adoption rates with velocity calculations, the analysis helps identify skills that are gaining traction in the job market and may become increasingly valuable in the future.
